# Bryan Archie — Portfolio Website

Single-page portfolio. Pure HTML/CSS/JS. No frameworks.

## Files

- `index.html` — all the content/sections
- `style.css` — all styling
- `script.js` — driving animation, scroll reveal, mobile nav
- `profile.jpg` — your photo (replace placeholder)
- `cv.pdf` — your CV (replace placeholder)

## To preview locally

Just double-click `index.html` and it opens in your browser. That's it.

## To customize

- **Photo**: replace `profile.jpg` with your own photo (same filename, square crop best)
- **CV**: replace `cv.pdf` with your actual CV PDF (same filename)
- **Text**: open `index.html` in VS Code, edit any text you want — it's all there in plain English
- **Colors**: at the top of `style.css` there's a `:root` block with variables. Change `--cyan`, `--magenta` etc. to recolor the site
